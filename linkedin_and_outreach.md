## 2. Cold Outreach Templates

Do not just apply to jobs. Since you are in Pakistan looking for global remote work, your highest conversion rate will come from reaching out directly to hiring managers, AI startup founders, and engineering leads.

### Strategy:
1. Use LinkedIn to search for **"Head of Engineering"**, **"ML Lead"**, **"Data Science Manager"**, or **"Founder"** at remote-first companies (especially series A/B startups using Wellfound).
2. Connect with them and add a personalized note.
3. If they accept, send a concise, high-impact message demonstrating your projects and value.

---

### Template 1: For LinkedIn Connection Request (300 Character Limit)
> Hi [Name], I recently transitioned 25 years of Air Force systems supervisor experience into Machine Learning engineering. I’ve built deployed projects in Credit Risk modeling (XGBoost/94% recall) & Healthcare Premium regression. I’d love to connect & discuss remote ML opportunities at [Company].
